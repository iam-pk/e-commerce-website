✦ Our Story

A Homemaker's Journey
into Creative Freedom

What started as a quiet corner of her home filled with colourful fabrics and a sewing needle has blossomed into something truly beautiful. Meet the heart behind Taar se Taar — a homemaker, a mother, and now, an artisan.

With no formal training and boundless love for fabric, she began crafting small household items — first for her own kitchen, then for friends and family. Today, every roti cover, every pooja aasan, every little hair bow carries the warmth of her hands and the quiet joy of doing what she loves.

Her message is simple: creativity has no age, no qualification, no deadline. If something calls to your heart, pick it up and begin. The thread will find its way.
